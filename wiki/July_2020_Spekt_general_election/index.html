The July 2020 Spekt general election was held on Friday 10 July 2020. This was the first such election to be held with newly formed All-Spekt National Congress.

Date of the election[edit]

The deadline for candidate nominations was 4 July 2020, with political campaigning for six days until polling day on 10 July. On the day of the election, polling stations across the country were open from midnight UTC+00:00, and closed on 12 July at 23:59 UTC+00:00.

Voting eligibility[edit]

Individuals eligible to vote had to be a citizen of the Area 51 of the Spekt Bosniaks: Pro-B_CD Trans Talk, Memorial Cult, and Peter Falk Fanclub before 10 July 2020.

Contesting political parties and candidates[edit]

Most candidates are representatives of a political party, which must be registered with the Electoral Commission's Register of Political Parties. Candidates who do not belong to a registered party can use an "independent" label. Across the Area 51 of the Spekt Bosniaks: Pro-B_CD Trans Talk, Memorial Cult, and Peter Falk Fanclub, there were 11 candidates representing 5 political parties.
